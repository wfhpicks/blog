# What to Look For

When shopping for a budget microphone for meetings, there are several key factors to consider. First, look for a microphone with good sound quality, which is essential for clear communication. A cheap USB microphone with poor sound quality can be frustrating to use and may not be effective for meetings. Second, consider the connectivity options, such as USB or wireless connectivity, and ensure that they are compatible with your computer or device. Third, think about the durability and build quality of the microphone, as a budget microphone that is poorly made may not last long. Finally, consider the price and ensure that it fits within your budget, as affordable mic options are available for work from home meetings.

# What to Look For

When shopping for a budget monitor home office, there are several key factors to consider. First, resolution is crucial - look for a monitor with at least 1080p (Full HD) for clear and crisp images. Second, screen size is important - a 22-24 inch monitor is a great size for most home offices, offering plenty of screen real estate without taking up too much space. Third, connectivity options are essential - ensure the monitor has the right ports to connect to your computer, such as HDMI, VGA, and USB. Finally, consider the monitor's adjustability and comfort features, such as tilt, swivel, and height adjustment, to ensure you can work comfortably for long periods.
